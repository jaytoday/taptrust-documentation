## What is TapTrust?

TapTrust is an AI assistant designed from the ground up with privacy as the core principle. Unlike traditional AI services where your conversations are stored and processed on company servers, TapTrust ensures that only you can access your conversations.

Your messages are encrypted on your device, and the AI processing happens in secure, isolated environments that ensures that only you have access to your chat messages and data.

### Easy To Access From Any Device

Unlike other privacy oriented AI assistants, TapTrust does not require you to run your own local AI model or complete any technical setup. 

Simply login on any device with your Google account, phone number or email address to start new private chats and access previous private chats.
